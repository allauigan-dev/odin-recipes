Sinigang na Baboy

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork (belly or ribs)
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish (daikon), sliced
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 1 cup water spinach (kangkong)
  • 2-3 green chili peppers
  • 1/4 cup tamarind paste or fresh tamarind
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, boil the water and add the pork, onion, and tomatoes.
  2. Simmer until the pork is tender, about 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Add the radish and green beans, cooking for another 5-10 minutes.
  4. Stir in the tamarind paste and green chili peppers.
  5. Finally, add the water spinach and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.

Notes

Sinigang can be made with various proteins, such as shrimp or fish, and can be adjusted for sourness based on personal preference.
