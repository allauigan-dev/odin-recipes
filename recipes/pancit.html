Pancit Canton

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pancit canton noodles
  • 1 lb chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 cup shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak the pancit canton noodles in hot water for about 10 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and onion, saut√©ing until fragrant.
  4. Add the chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  5. Stir in the shrimp, carrots, and cabbage, cooking until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Add the soaked noodles, soy sauce, and oyster sauce, tossing everything together.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  8. Garnish with green onions before serving.

Notes

Pancit can be customized with different proteins and vegetables based on preference.
