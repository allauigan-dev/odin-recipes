Adobo

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken or pork
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns in a bowl.
  2. Add the chicken or pork and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. In a pot, add the marinated meat and the marinade, along with water.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes.
  5. Season with salt to taste and serve with rice.

Notes

Adobo can be made with various meats and can be adjusted to taste.
